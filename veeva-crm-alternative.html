Alternative guide

A lightweight Veeva CRM alternative — for the targeting part only

Veeva CRM (now Vault CRM) is the industry-standard commercial platform for life sciences, and if you need field-force workflows, validated compliance tooling, and Veeva OpenData's verified HCP universe, it's the right tool. But many teams adopt — or inherit — an enterprise CRM when the job in front of them is much smaller: turn HCP lists into scored, compliant, exportable segments. That's the entire job LiteHCP Targeter does.

Who switches (or starts) with LiteHCP

  • Agencies and consultancies building target lists for multiple pharma clients without reselling enterprise seats.
  • Regional affiliates and small market teams whose HQ owns the CRM but who still need local segmentation discipline.
  • Early-stage and specialty companies that aren't ready for a CRM implementation but need defensible targeting now.
  • Data-residency-sensitive teams that want HCP data on their own infrastructure, not a vendor cloud.
deploy in minutes — upload litehcp.php to any PHP 8 host, register, click Load sample, and you're looking at 200 scored profiles, 8 rules, and 5 segments. Your own CSVs import the same way.

What you give up

Honesty matters here: LiteHCP has no verified HCP database, no field-force tooling, no omnichannel execution, and no vendor compliance certifications. It produces clean, audited segment exports that feed whatever execution tools you already use. If your organization requires a validated enterprise stack end-to-end, use the enterprise stack.
