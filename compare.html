Honest comparison

You probably don't need an enterprise suite to build a target list.

Platforms like Veeva CRM, Salesforce Life Sciences Cloud, and IQVIA OCE are excellent at what they do — running the entire commercial operation of a life-sciences company. LiteHCP Targeter does one slice of that job: turning HCP CSVs into scored, compliant, exportable segments. If that slice is all you need, the difference in cost and complexity is enormous.

Which one is right for you?

Choose LiteHCP Targeter

You need clean segments, not a platform

  • Your HCP data arrives as CSVs from conferences, partners, data vendors, or your own systems.
  • You're a small team, agency, or regional affiliate without enterprise CRM budget or admins.
  • Data residency matters — you want HCP data on infrastructure you control.
  • You want export discipline: minimal fields by default, PII gated and audited.
  • You need it working this week, on hosting you already pay for.
Choose an enterprise platform

You're running the whole commercial engine

  • You need verified HCP reference data (Veeva OpenData, IQVIA OneKey) kept current at scale.
  • Field reps need call planning, sampling, and territory management.
  • You execute email and omnichannel campaigns inside the same system.
  • You have compliance requirements that mandate a specific validated vendor stack.
  • You have the budget, admins, and implementation timeline to match.
